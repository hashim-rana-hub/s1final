The Los Angeles Times is the largest metropolitan daily newspaper in the country, with a daily readership of 1.3 million and 2 million on Sunday,more than 30 million unique latimes.com visitors monthly and a combined print and online local weekly audience of 4.6 million. The Pulitzer Prize-winning Times has been covering Southern California for more than 138 years. Los Angeles Times’ businesses and affiliates also include The Envelope, Hot Property, Times Community News and Los Angeles Times en Español. The Times also owns California Community News, LLC.
